# Code of Conduct

## Standard

Be kind. Be specific. Ship something.

We follow the
[Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
The full text governs.

## Unacceptable

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions.

## Enforcement

Report violations to **conduct@recall.works**. Reports are confidential.
Maintainers will investigate and respond within seven days. Consequences range
from a private warning to a permanent ban from project spaces, proportionate
to the severity and pattern of behavior.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, chat) and to public
representations of the project (conference talks, social posts tagged with the
project, etc.).
